Intro

Pebble is clast of rock with a particle size of 2 to 64 millimeters based on the Krumbein phi scale of sedimentology. Pebbles are used for water purification, sewage treatment, electricity, garden items. Also supply the excellent raw auxiliary material for ceramics, precision casting, paper, sprayed grinding, chemicals, silicon, national defense, etc.

Crushing Processing Flow

The raw material is fed regularly by the vibrating feeder to the primary jaw and secondary jaw/cone crusher for crushing. The crushed material is then transported by belt conveyor to the following vibrating screen for separation. Proper sizes (usually there would be an upper limit for different model of sand making machinery) would be transported to the sand making machine directly, while unrequired materials will be returned to the secondary jaw/cone crusher for repeated crushing until getting required sizes. The crushed material from the sand making machine will be transported to/ returned to the vibrating screen for final separation. Required sand size product would be returned to the sand washing machine for clearance, and then retained as final product. Unrequested product will be returned to the sand making machine for repeated crushing until getting all required size.

Application

KEFID 150tph sandmaking line in Ghana

MATERIAL: Pebble

OUTPUT SIZE: 0-5-13-19mm

MAX FEED SIZE: 630mm

CAPACITY: 150t/h

Necessary Equipments

HPT Cone Crusher

Stationary Crushers

HPT Cone Crusher

Process Description

1). Hopper. There will be a hopper made in your local site upper the vibrating feeder for collecting materials.

2). The material will be fed into the primary jaw crusher PE750X1060 (Max input size 630MM) by vibrating feeder continuously and crushed to below 150mm.

3). After crushed by primary jaw crushing plant, the materials below 150mm will be transported to the surge hopper to stock. There will be small feeder GZG110-4 to feed the secondary crusher CS cone crusher CSB160/C, which can give 200tph passing capacity.

4).The crushed stone will go to the vibrating screen 2YZS1860, and +40mm will return to the cone crusher, and 0-40mm will go to the secondary surge hopper to stock. The feeder GZG110-4 will feed the tertiary VSI 9532 vertical shaft impact crusher. This crusher will give you the good quality and gradation aggregates, very suitable for ready mix plants.

5) There is another vibrating screen 3YZS2160 after the VSI 9532, the +19mm will return to the secondary surge hopper, and the 0-5-13-19mm will be screened out as final products.
